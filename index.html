Citizen are two people who thought they should provide a combination of over 20 years of agency and charity experience, for charities who need the support, but don't want to pay the big agency fees.

We were born just over a year ago. In that time our little collective has managed to work on some great causes. We launched the new lottery product for Amnesty, asked Farm Africa supporters to be part of ending hunger in Africa, made the supporters of Birmingham Children's Hospital 'Super Santas' for giving the gift of Christmas to children with cancer and helped the Refugee Council protect Syrian refugees who have made it to the UK.
